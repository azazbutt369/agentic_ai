# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors, maintainers, and community members pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

* Age
* Body size
* Disability
* Ethnicity
* Gender identity and expression
* Level of experience
* Nationality
* Personal appearance
* Race
* Religion
* Sexual identity and orientation
* Technical background

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and collaborative environment.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and constructive
* Providing helpful feedback
* Being open to different viewpoints
* Supporting newcomers
* Sharing knowledge generously
* Taking responsibility for mistakes
* Focusing on what benefits the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination
* Personal attacks
* Trolling or insulting comments
* Publishing private information without consent
* Deliberately disruptive behavior
* Plagiarism or misrepresentation of work
* Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project

---

## Community Expectations

This repository exists to help people learn and build modern AI systems.

When participating:

* Assume good intentions.
* Critique ideas, not people.
* Use evidence and reasoning when disagreeing.
* Be professional in discussions.
* Help maintain a welcoming environment.

---

## Open Source Collaboration

We encourage:

* Questions from beginners
* Documentation improvements
* Resource sharing
* Project submissions
* Research discussions
* Constructive technical debate

No one should feel discouraged from contributing because of their experience level.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

* Clarifying community standards
* Reviewing reports fairly
* Taking appropriate action when necessary
* Protecting contributors from harassment

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, pull requests, issues, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Open a private discussion with maintainers.
2. Provide relevant details.
3. Include links or screenshots when applicable.

All reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially whenever possible.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

### Level 1 — Warning

Used for:

* Minor disruptive behavior
* Inappropriate language
* Unconstructive interactions

Action:

* Private warning
* Request for behavior correction

---

### Level 2 — Temporary Restriction

Used for:

* Repeated violations
* Hostile interactions
* Harassment

Action:

* Temporary suspension from participation

---

### Level 3 — Permanent Ban

Used for:

* Severe harassment
* Hate speech
* Threats
* Deliberate community disruption

Action:

* Permanent removal from the project community

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Documentation contributions
* Community spaces associated with this project
